Tinkering Lab

Tinkering Lab is a technical facility open to all the students of Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee. Here you can go beyond your curriculum and have an opportunity to explore the various technological advancements that 21st century has seen and get inspired to start tinkering, building and innovating for converting creative ideas into actual engineering products.
