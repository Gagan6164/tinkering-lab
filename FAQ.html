• What are the timing for the lab?


    The Lab timings are from 12 Noon – 8pm from Monday to Friday. Currently, the lab is operational for 5 days a week.

  • What procedure do I need to follow to access the Lab’s Facilities?


    Students’ have to fill a form which requires the information of the project. The form is available on the website in the forms section.

  • Can I use the lab for personal use


    If your personal use is a personal project you are most welcome. Tinkering Lab doesn’t appreciate any kind of misuse of the Lab and its facilities to cater personnel needs.

  • What are the facilities present at the Lab?


    The Tinkering Lab is equipped with numerous technologies including 3D printing, laser etching, carving and with number of workstations.

  • I don’t know how to use the machines so who will help me to use them


    The lab officials and students’ body is meant to guide the students in their projects. They are always available for the help. Also, over a period of time, you will develop the skills of using these machines.

  • Who all can access the lab?


    The lab is present to cater the need of students and professors of IIT Roorkee. Any member of IIT Roorkee can access the lab for the projects.

  • Are there any prerequisites for working in the lab?


    No, there are no such prerequisites for working in the lab but we appreciate if you come in formals to keep yourself safe from any kind of miss happening in the lab.

  • Whom can I contact for further queries


    Tinkering Lab is always present at your service. For further queries, feel free to drop an email at rethink.tinkering@gmail.com .
